Welcome to my site!

[English]

我目前担任北京上添影视传媒有限公司的流程研发TD，负责色彩制作流程研发、软件开发、技术标准制定和测试。 2020年我曾是中国科学院计算技术研究所的研发实习生。

我将于2024年6月在北京电影学院中国电影高新技术研究院完成硕士学位。 我本科毕业于北京电影学院影视技术系。 2021年和2022年我担任计算机图形学课程的助教。 我曾2020和2023两次获得教育部国家奖学金，并2018-2024年七次获得学术一等奖学金。 我的研究兴趣是电影智能化制作、角色运动和色彩科学。

此外，我还是广告和短片等项目的自由调色师和剪辑师，完成商业广告和短片百余部。

[中文]

I am currently working as a Technical Director of Beijing Shangtian Media, responsible for the development of color production pipelines, software development, and technical standard development and testing. During my career, I was fortunate enough to be an R&D intern at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2020.

I will complete my M.A degree from the China Film High-tech Research Institute at Beijing Film Academy in June 2024. I received my B.F.A. degree from the Film and Television Technology Department at Beijing Film Academy. I was the TA of the Computer Graphics course in 2021 and 2022. I have received the Ministry of Education National Scholarship twice, in the years 2020 and 2023, and have also been awarded the first-class academic scholarship seven times from 2018 to 2024. My research interests are in intelligent film production, character motion and color science.

In addition, I work as freelance colorist and editor on commercials and short film projects.
