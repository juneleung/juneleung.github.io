[English]

我目前是惠灵顿维多利亚大学的博士生，研究方向为角色动画和运动重定向。研究由惠灵顿博士奖学金资助。

在此之前， 我在中国电影高科技研究院和北京电影学院影视技术系获得了硕士和学士学位。 我获得了中国教育部国家奖学金（2020、2023）、北京市优秀毕业生（2021、2024）和北京电影学院优秀毕业生（2021、2024）。

在我的职业生涯中，我有幸于2020年在中国科学院计算技术研究所实习。我从2020年开始担任北京上添影视传媒的流程研发TD。

我的研究兴趣是电影智能化制作、角色运动和色彩科学。

如果您对项目合作感兴趣，包括但不限于研究项目、电影制作和技术开发，请通过 juneleungchan@gmail.com 与我联系。

[中文]

I am currently a PhD student at Victoria University of Wellington, focusing on character animation and motion retargeting. Research is funded by the Wellington Doctoral Scholarship.

Before that, I obtained the M.A and the B.F.A. degree at the China Film High-tech Research Institute and Film & Television Technology Department at Beijing Film Academy. I'm a recipient of the National Scholarship of the Ministry of Education of China(2020, 2023), Outstanding Graduate of Beijing(2021, 2024), and the Outstanding Graduate of the Beijing Film Academy(2021, 2024).

During my career, I was fortunate to intern at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2020. I worked as a Technical Director of Beijing Shangtian Media from 2020.

My research interests are in intelligent film production, character motion, and color science.

If you are interested in project cooperation, including but not limited to research projects, film production, and technology development, please feel free to contact me at juneleungchan@gmail.com.
