I am currently working as a Technical Director of Beijing Shangtian Media, responsible for the development of color production pipelines, software development, and technical standard development and testing.

I obtained the M.A degree at the China Film High-tech Research Institute at Beijing Film Academy and the B.F.A. degree at the Film and Television Technology Department at Beijing Film Academy. In 2021 and 2024, I was awarded the Outstanding Graduate of Beijing and the Outstanding Graduate of the Beijing Film Academy. I have won the National Scholarship of the Ministry of Education twice in 2020 and 2023, and the First-Class Academic Scholarship seven times from 2018 to 2024. I was the TA of the Computer Graphics course in 2021 and 2022. During my career, I was fortunate enough to be an R&D intern at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2020. My research interests are in intelligent film production, character motion and color science.

In addition, I work as freelance colorist and editor on commercials and short film projects.

If you are interested in project cooperation, including but not limited to research projects, film production, and technology development, please feel free to contact me.
